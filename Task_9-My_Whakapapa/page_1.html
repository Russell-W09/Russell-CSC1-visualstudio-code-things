Chinese culture

China is rich in tradition, philosophy, and beliefs spanning over thousands of years. Festivals such as the Lunar New Year and Mid-Autumn Festival are often celebrated with family, marking the start of the new year, or unity and completeness. Chinese philosophy focuses on harmony, human nature, and balance, being influenced from Confucianism, Daoism, and Buddhism. China's diverse religious landscape include Buddhism, Taoism, Islam, Protestantism, and Catholicism.
