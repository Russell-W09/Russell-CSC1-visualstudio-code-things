Why Chess?

It's a fun board game you can play with another person and has many strategies you can choose to challenge your opponents with. From basic trades to the rewarding 'checkmate', chess is a place to refine your skills, from strategic thinking, focus, to creativity.

Join The Community

Connect with fellow OSC students who share your passion for chess. Practice matches, participate in tournaments, discuss strategies, and learn together. Our supportive community creates the perfect environment for chess enthusiasts to grow, challenge themselves, and develop lifelong skills through chess.
